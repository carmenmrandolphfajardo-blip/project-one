"Hill House"

  • Case #3367
  • Classification: Geographical
  • Status: Safe

Disturbance Report - 001

Foundation became aware of the anomalous site hereby referred to as "Hill House" in 1952, after a scholarly paper by Dr. John Montague was published detailing the events of his stay in the summer of 1951. Foundation personnel conducted a preliminary investigation and subsequently acquired the property from the Sanderson family. The site located is located 20 miles north of [REDACTED]. Local population has been deemed a non-issue to the security of the site.

Hill House c. 1909

Excerpt from the journal of Dr. John Montague

"The house is a most peculiar structure, with angles that seem to shift and change when one is not looking directly at them. The atmosphere within is oppressive, and I have experienced several unsettling phenomena during my stay here. I am convinced that the house itself is alive in some manner, and I intend to uncover its secrets."

Security protocol:

  • All personnel must undergo a psychological evaluation before and after visiting the site.
  • No electronic devices are allowed within 100 meters of the property.
  • Any anomalous activity must be reported immediately to site command.
